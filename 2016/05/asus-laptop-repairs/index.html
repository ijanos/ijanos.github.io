ASUS laptop repairs

May 14, 2016
soldering asus

My sister has an ASUS K43 laptop that started misbehaving. Failing to detect and charge when the power cord was plugged in. Warranty long gone, the response in repair shops was the classic “we have to change the motherboard”. Unsatisfied with that answer I found out that the power socket is a common fault in these ASUS machines and you can order replacement sockets for less than a US dollar on ebay. Being a true millennial I watched three youtube videos, one about taking apart the laptop and two about desoldering. I bought some desoldering wick, borrowed a soldering station. If I screw up I can still pay for a replacement motherboard.
